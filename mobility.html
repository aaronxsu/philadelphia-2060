M2: Improve Neighborhood Surface Transit

Interactive surface transit map

The planned enhanced surface transit network will primarily run on major arterial streets and will knit the city together. The goal for service along these corridor is autonomous, high capacity vehicles that run frequently, ranging from every 3 minutes at peak in Tier 1 corridors to every 7-10 minutes at peak in Tier 3 corridors.

Tier 1 routes function as essentially rapid transit in their own right, with Tier 2 and 3 routes largely serving connections to rail based rapid transit stations.

M3: Transform Regional Rail

Interactive Urban Rail Map

The Urban Rail program will invest strategically in ADA improvements, vehicle purchases, and other measures needed to improve frequencies to below 15 minutes or less.

This will transform the mobility of northern neighborhoods and the ability of city residents to reach new opportunities around the region.
